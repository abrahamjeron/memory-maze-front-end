Instructions for the game:

The goal of MemoryMaze is to test and improve your memory skills by matching pairs of images within a specified time limit and move count.


1.Choose a difficulty level: easy, medium, or difficult.
2.Choose a difficulty level: easy, medium, or difficult.
3.You have a specific time limit to complete each level.
4.In the easy level, find all pairs within the time limit and 12 moves.
5.In the medium , locate matching pairs within the time limit and 23 moves.
6. In the difficult level,locate matching pairs within the time you have 40 moves.
